FAQ

Your Questions,
Answered.

Have more questions? Call us at +92 300 000 0000 or WhatsApp us any time — we respond within the hour.

Is Moonsoon Minerals water safe for drinking and cooking? +
Absolutely. Our water passes through a 7-stage purification process including RO membrane filtration, UV sterilisation, and mineral balancing. It is tested in PSQCA-accredited laboratories and meets all WHO drinking water quality standards. It is completely safe for drinking, cooking, baby formula preparation, and medical use.
What is the minimum order for custom label water bottles? +
Our minimum order for custom labelled bottles is 500 units. We offer sizes in 250ml, 500ml, 1 litre, and 1.5 litre. The price includes a free label design service. Delivery is 5–7 working days. Larger orders (5,000+) qualify for special rates.
How much does an RO plant installation cost? +
Every RO plant is custom-designed for your water quality, required output capacity, and site conditions. We offer a completely free site assessment and no-obligation quote. Systems typically range from PKR 150,000 for a small commercial unit to PKR 2,000,000+ for large industrial plants. We also offer flexible payment plans.
How often is the water tested and can I see the reports? +
Every production batch is tested internally before dispatch. We also conduct monthly independent laboratory tests at a PSQCA-accredited facility. All customers can request the latest lab report at any time. We also display current quality certificates on this website. Transparency is central to everything we do.
What areas of Karachi do you cover? +
We currently deliver to all major areas of Karachi including DHA, Clifton, PECHS, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, North Nazimabad, Saddar, Malir, Landhi, Korangi and surrounding areas. For RO plant installations, we cover all of Sindh. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location.
Do you offer annual maintenance contracts for RO plants? +
Yes. Our AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) covers scheduled filter replacements, membrane inspection and cleaning, pump servicing, quarterly water quality testing, and a guaranteed 4-hour emergency call-out response. AMC packages start from PKR 24,000 per year depending on plant size and complexity.
